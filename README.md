## How to Use

1. Donwload The Files
1. Archvie on Xcode
1. Register with Login Items on System Preferences(Users & Groups)
1. Open the App
1. Just Listening to Favorite Songs

### How to Archive on Xcode

1. [Product]
1. [Archive]
1. [Distribute App]
1. Select "Custom"
1. Select "Copy App"
1. [Export]
